Our Mission

From our experience as college students, we understand how tough it can be to find an ideal roommate. We believe that having roommate(s) with whom you share similar interests and values has a postive influence on one's overall life experence and may result in strong bonds and potential lifelong friendships. Although we may not guarantee that Cougar Roomie will offer just that, we are doing our best to facilitate the process of finding roommates within our student community at CSUSM.
